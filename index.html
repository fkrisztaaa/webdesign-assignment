Why choose Media Mindset Camp?

Our program is designed for high school students aged 14-18 who are curious about:

Media Literacy: Understanding the messages behind the media.

Social Media Savvy: Building skills to use social platforms effectively.

Content Creation: Crafting engaging videos, posts, and stories.

Digital Ethics: Exploring online responsibility and privacy.

What’s Waiting for You?

At Media Mindset Camp, you can expect a week filled with hands-on learning, creativity, and exciting challenges. Our carefully designed workshops will introduce you to the world of media creation, from writing and photography to video editing and social media strategy. You’ll have the opportunity to explore your passion for storytelling while developing practical skills that will serve you well in both school and future careers. Whether you're behind a camera or crafting the perfect caption, every activity is designed to help you build your confidence and creativity in the digital space.

But it's not just about learning—it’s about doing. Throughout the camp, you’ll work on real-world projects alongside like-minded peers, collaborating on content that could go viral or change how we view digital media. You’ll also hear from expert mentors who will guide you every step of the way, offering tips, feedback, and inspiration. By the end of the week, you'll leave with a portfolio of work, new skills, and an expanded network of fellow young media enthusiasts. Ready to dive into the world of media?
