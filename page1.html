Our Mission

In a world where digital media shapes how we think, connect, and communicate, Media Mindset Camp is here to ensure young minds are ready to lead. Our mission is to empower high schoolers with the skills and confidence to create meaningful content and navigate the digital world responsibly.

What Makes Us Unique?

At Media Mindset Camp, we combine expert mentorship with hands-on experiences to create a learning environment that’s as fun as it is impactful. Our workshops cover everything from spotting fake news to creating viral videos, giving you the tools to make a real difference online.

Learn. Create. Have Fun!

Media Mindset Camp is not your average summer camp—it’s a vibrant hub of creativity, collaboration, and discovery. We believe learning should be exciting and interactive, which is why our program blends practical skills with creative expression to keep you engaged every step of the way.

Preparing You for Tomorrow’s Challenges

The digital age is filled with opportunities—but also challenges. At Media Mindset Camp, we’re here to help you make the most of both. From understanding media influence to mastering content creation tools, our workshops are designed to give you real-world skills that matter.

Our Commitment to You:

We believe every student has the potential to become a smart, savvy, and creative media user. Our experienced mentors, engaging activities, and supportive environment ensure you’ll leave camp ready to shine in school, your career, and beyond.

Skills You’ll Gain at Media Mindset Camp

Learn media literacy to evaluate content critically, spot misinformation, and understand media’s societal impact. Develop creative writing, photography, and video editing techniques to craft engaging stories across platforms. Dive into social media strategy, building authentic brands and growing audiences, while mastering digital safety and ethical media use.
Our collaborative projects will enhance teamwork and communication, helping you confidently present your ideas and creations. Whether you're aiming to become a content creator or simply navigate the online space responsibly, these skills will empower your journey.

Got Questions?

Who can attend Media Mindset Camp?

The camp is open to high school students aged 14-18 who are curious about media, content creation, and online communication. No prior experience is required—just a passion for learning!

Where and when does the camp take place?

The camp runs from 13th to 19th of July, 2025 at Cluj Napoca. It’s a week-long program filled with interactive workshops, engaging activities, and plenty of hands-on practice.

What should I bring to the camp?

You’ll need a smartphone or laptop (if available) for activities like photography and video editing. We provide other professional equipment to try out. Don’t forget to bring comfortable clothing, a notebook, pens, and lots of enthusiasm!

Are meals and accommodations provided?

Yes, the camp fee includes daily meals (breakfast, lunch, and snacks). If accommodations are required, please contact us to discuss available options.

Do I need to have any prior experience with media or content creation?

Not at all! The camp is designed to be beginner-friendly, with all workshops tailored to help you learn step by step. Whether you're new to media or already experimenting, there’s something for everyone.

What safety measures are in place during the camp?

We prioritize the safety and well-being of our participants. The venue is supervised by trained staff, and all equipment is sanitized regularly. Additionally, our online safety sessions include practical tips to keep you secure in the digital world.
