Our Mentors

Emily Carter

Role: Creative Writing & Content Development Mentor

About Emily:
Emily is a published author and digital content creator with a knack for turning ideas into captivating stories. With over 5 years of experience as a content strategist for major blogs and online magazines, she knows how to create content that resonates with audiences.

Workshops:

The Power of Words: Crafting compelling narratives for blogs, posts, and campaigns.
Storytelling for Social Media: Writing engaging captions and scripts for Instagram, TikTok, and YouTube.
Finding Your Voice: Unlocking your unique style as a writer.

Fun Fact: Emily believes everyone has a story to tell and loves helping students find theirs through creativity and collaboration.

Sophia Ramirez

Role: Online Media & Social Media Mentor

About Sophia:
With a background in digital marketing and social media strategy, Sophia has worked with startups and influencers to build successful online presences. She’s an advocate for responsible digital citizenship and enjoys mentoring young creators.

Workshops:

Building Your Brand: Designing a personal or professional identity online.
Social Media Secrets: Tips for growing an authentic audience on major platforms.
Staying Safe Online: Understanding privacy settings, online ethics, and digital detox strategies.

Fun Fact: Sophia is a huge fan of memes and often uses them as teaching tools in her sessions.

Liam Novak

Role: Photography & Video Editing Mentor

About Liam:
An award-winning freelance photographer and video editor, Liam specializes in creating visually striking content for brands and independent projects. His work has been featured in online campaigns, short films, and exhibitions around the globe.

Workshops:

Photography Basics: Tips and tricks for capturing stunning shots using your phone or camera.
Mastering Visual Storytelling: How to plan, shoot, and edit short videos that grab attention.
From Pixels to Perfect: Basic video editing techniques for platforms like YouTube and TikTok.

Fun Fact: Liam's favorite challenge is teaching students to turn ordinary objects into extraordinary visuals.

The CEO's message:

"In today’s fast-paced digital world, understanding and mastering media is no longer optional—it’s essential. At Media Mindset Camp, we’re not just teaching skills; we’re shaping a mindset. Our goal is to help students develop the critical thinking, creativity, and ethical awareness they need to thrive online and offline.

Whether you’re passionate about creating videos, writing stories, or simply making sense of the media around you, this camp is for you. Our team of expert mentors and interactive workshops ensure that every student leaves with practical knowledge and the confidence to make a positive impact in the digital space.

I wholeheartedly recommend Media Mindset Camp to any high schooler ready to take charge of their digital future. Let’s unlock your potential together!"
